Voice Truthgate

Is this voice real or AI? Honest, on-device. Your audio never leaves your browser.

Record or upload a short clip to get an instant band.

This is a demo. The instant check is a minimal illustrative heuristic — the real one lives in @mosadd/voice-analyzer-core. The stronger check downloads a real model (as1605/Deepfake-audio-detection-V2, ~379 MB) and runs it in your browser. See the repository and each package's model card.
